about us

Grassroots action for meaningful social change

Established in 2018, Aanav Seva Foundation is a community-focused NGO headquartered in Pune, Maharashtra. We work at the grassroots to address urgent social issues.

Our Core Impact Programs

Aanav Seva Foundation executes sustainable, grassroots initiatives focusing on women's health, child nutrition, environmental care, and cultural preservation.

Poshan Aahar (Child Nutrition)

Combating child malnutrition and supporting education by serving daily nutritious tiffins to 80+ special and needy children.

read more

Aditi (Women's Health & Hygiene)

Distributing sanitary napkins free of cost and organizing hygiene awareness camps for over 2,500 rural women in Maharashtra.

read more

Kalpavruksha (Environment)

Urban greening and carbon reduction through bamboo plantation in biodiversity zones, planting 300+ trees with local involvement.

what we do

Empowering Communities, Restoring Nature

Women's Health & Menstrual Hygiene

Conducting health check-up camps and workshops in rural areas, distribution of free sanitary napkins, and training peer health educators.

Environmental Sustainability

Planting high oxygen-releasing bamboo saplings in biodiversity zones to absorb carbon dioxide and engage youth in nature conservation.

Child Nutrition & Welfare

Serving daily hygienic, nutritious meals and tiffins to needy and special children to support their growth, health, and school attendance.
