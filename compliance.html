Organizational Legitimacy

Rigorous Standards, Complete Compliance

Established in 2018, Aanav Seva Foundation is a community-focused, legally registered Section 8 Non-Profit Organization headquartered in Pune, Maharashtra. We operate under strict corporate compliance frameworks, ensuring 100% financial transparency and strict alignment with Ministry of Corporate Affairs Section 135 rules.

Whether collaborating with individual philanthropists or executing flagship CSR campaigns with partners like the JSW Foundation, we safeguard corporate and private funds through meticulous utilization tracking and open legal disclosure.
