About Us 
 
Established in 2018, Aanav Seva Foundation is a community-focused NGO headquartered 
in Pune, Maharashtra. We work at the grassroots to address urgent social issues in the fields 
of women’s health , environmental sustainability, cultural enrichment , and child 
nutrition & education. 
 
Our foundation is built on service, sincerity, and sustainability — not just in words, but in 
tangible, life-changing action. We believe that meaningful change begins at the grassroots 
and that every human deserves dignity, access, and opportunity.

 Vision 
To create a just, inclusive, compassionate , and united  society where health, environment, 
culture, and nourishment are the shared rights of every individual — not privileges of a few. 
 
 
 
 Mission 
To serve marginalized and underserved communities with sustainable programs that 
empower individuals, protect the environment, preserve heritage, and support basic health 
and nutrition needs through collaborative partnerships and community engagement. 
 
 
 Goal 
To be a catalyst for systemic, positive change in society through innovative and impactful 
programs, partnerships, and people -centric initiatives, improving the quality of life for 
present and future generations. 
 
 
 Aim & Objectives 
 
Aims: 
 To uplift underprivileged communities through access to basic education, health, 
nutrition, and hygiene. 
 To create sustainable models of environmental stewardship and green practices. 
 To promote and protect India’s rich cultural and artistic legacy. 
 To foster inclusive development through education and community empowerment. 
 
Objectives: 
1. Conduct health check -up and hyg iene awareness programs for rural women and 
children. 
2. Provide free or subsidized sanitary napkins and nutritional meals to the underserved. 
3. Initiate tree plantation and carbon reduction projects in urban and semi-urban areas. 
4. Organize cultural events to preserve classical Indian art forms. 
5. Engage youth and volunteers in sustainable and inclusive development activities. 
6. Promote partnerships with government bodies, corporates, and civil society for 
greater impact. 
7. Ensure transparent, ethical, and community-led program execution.

 Our Impact – Highlights from the Field 
 
🎭 Nrutyotsava 
 
Celebrating Classical Indian Dance Heritage 
 Hosted a grand classical dance festival in Pune, commemorating the legacy of Guru 
Pandita Rohinitai Bhate. 
 Over 800 artists performed across forms like Kathak, Bharatanatyam, Odissi. 
 Guidance by Smt. Shama Bhate , Manishatai Sathe , Sucheta Chaphekar  and 
performances by Shambhavi Dandekar , Sharvari Jamenis , Rutuja Soman , and 
others. 
 Witnessed by 10,000+ audience members, rekindling passion for traditional arts. 
 To nurture the continuity of our rich cultural heritage and bequeathing legacy to 
future generations. 
✔ A milestone cultural initiative for Pune 
✔ Supported intergenerational learning in dance

🌿 Kalpavruksha 
 
Environmental Sustainability & Carbon Reduction 
 In response to Pune’s CO₂ levels and pollution, we planted Bamboo trees  in the 
Amenity and Biodiversity zones of Savitribai Phule Pune University. 
 Bamboo, being high in oxygen release and carbon absorption, was chosen for long -
term sustainability. 
 The initiative involved students and locals in environmental education and tree care. 
✔ 300+ saplings planted 
✔ Model project for green urban spaces

🌸 Aditi 
 
Women’s Health & Menstrual Hygiene Awareness 
 Carried out health check -up camps and hygiene workshops for women across rural 
Maharashtra. 
 Distributed sanitary napkins free of cost in 2019 and 2020. 
 COVID-19 Response:  
During lockdowns, when women had no access to sanitary products, we distributed 
napkins free of cost for six straight months, ensuring dignity and care in crisis. 
 
✔ Reached over 2,500 women 
✔ Empowered rural health workers and peer educators
