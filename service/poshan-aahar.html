The Poshan Aahar Program is a dedicated child nutrition initiative by the Aanav Seva Foundation, focused on combatting child malnutrition and supporting kids with special needs by providing daily, fresh, and nutritious hot tiffins in Pune. By working closely with local schools and grassroots NGOs, we ensure that the right food reaches the most vulnerable children.

Beyond addressing immediate hunger, Poshan Aahar focuses on balanced diet components, fresh kitchen hygiene, and consistent nutritional tracking. The program has shown visible positive impacts in local communities, leading to improved school attendance, better focus in class, and stronger growth indicators among our 80+ daily beneficiaries.

Nourishment & growth for every child

Frequently asked questions

We provide hot, fresh, hygienic, and balanced meals tailored specifically for child nutrition, including proteins, fresh vegetables, lentils, and essential grains.

The program currently supports 80+ special-needs and underprivileged needy children in local municipal schools and community centers around Pune.

We operate strict kitchen standards, utilizing purified water, fresh daily produce, stainless steel cooking utensils, and clean insulated packaging for our tiffin delivery service.
