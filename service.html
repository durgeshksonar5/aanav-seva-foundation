Answers to important your questions

To serve marginalized and underserved communities in Maharashtra with sustainable programs that empower individuals, protect the environment, preserve cultural heritage, and support basic health & nutrition needs.

You can join us by volunteering for our campaigns in Pune, donating to support our child nutrition tiffins and menstrual hygiene kits, or proposing corporate CSR collaborations. Visit our Contact Us page to get in touch.

Donations directly fund our grassroots initiatives: providing daily hygienic meals to special children (Poshan Aahar), distributing sanitary napkins and health workshops for rural women (Aditi), and planting bamboo trees (Kalpavruksha).

Yes, Aanav Seva Foundation is registered under Section 12AA and 80G of the Income Tax Act. All donations qualify for tax deduction benefits as per Indian tax laws.

You can volunteer with us by participating in our local environmental planting drives, supporting our health check-up camps, or helping coordinate our classical dance festivals (Nrutyotsava). Please contact us at info@aanavfoundation.org to sign up.
