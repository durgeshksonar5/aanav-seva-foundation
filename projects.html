JSW Foundation Menstrual Hygiene Management (MHM) Program

The JSW Foundation MHM Program serves as our flagship execution model. Under this strategic corporate partnership, Aanav Seva Foundation deployed a highly coordinated grassroots operation to combat menstrual taboos and establish sustainable hygiene access for school girls and women across rural municipal zones.

Our team handled end-to-end execution, securing administrative approvals, conducting baseline community surveys, providing medical practitioner-led workshops, and building local sanitary storage and safe incinerator networks to ensure seamless disposal.

Verifiable Project Deliverables:

10,000+ Women Reached 50,000+ Napkins Distributed 20+ Municipal Schools 98% Adaptation Rate
