about us

Seva with Soul, Impact with Integrity

Established in 2018 in Pune, Maharashtra, Aanav Seva Foundation is a community-first Non-Government Organization (NGO) registered as a Section 8 Non-Profit entity. We work at the grassroots to create a just, inclusive, compassionate, and united society. By addressing urgent social challenges across Maharashtra, we focus on women's health and hygiene, environmental sustainability, children's nutrition & welfare, and classical art preservation.

Framework for Impact

Our Aims, Objectives & Guiding Beliefs

Aanav Seva Foundation operates under a strict, community-first framework built to deliver maximum social impact, transparency, and grassroots sustainability.

Aims & Objectives

Aims:

  • Uplift underprivileged communities via access to basic education, health, nutrition, and hygiene.
  • Create sustainable models of environmental stewardship and green urban practices.
  • Promote and protect India’s rich cultural and classical artistic dance heritage.

Core Objectives:

  • 1. Conduct healthcare check-up and menstrual hygiene awareness programs for rural women and children.
  • 2. Provide free or subsidized sanitary napkins and nutritional meals to the underserved.
  • 3. Plant bamboo tree saplings for long-term carbon reduction and biodiversity zones.
  • 4. Organize large-scale dance festivals (Nrutyotsava) to preserve Indian classical arts.
  • 5. Partner with government, corporate sponsors, and civil bodies to scale social impact.

Guiding Beliefs

Community-First Approach:

The community is not a passive beneficiary — it is our active partner in change.

Local Action, Global Thinking:

Every act of grassroots kindness contributes to a better, more united world.

Empowerment Through Participation:

Real, lasting development happens when people participate in decisions that affect them.

Action Over Rhetoric:

We believe in doing, not just speaking — because every grassroots effort counts.

Faith in Grassroots:

Solutions co-created at the grassroots level are the most resilient, ethical, and sustainable.

Statutory Registrations & Legal Standing
