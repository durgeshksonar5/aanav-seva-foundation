Aanav Seva Foundation – Website Content & CSR Positioning Document
This document contains the recommended website structure, positioning strategy, and content direction for Aanav Seva Foundation with a primary focus on attracting CSR partnerships and corporate funding.
Website Positioning Strategy
The website should position Aanav Seva Foundation as a credible grassroots implementation organization capable of executing scalable social impact programs across multiple sectors.
Core Identity
Aanav Seva Foundation should be presented as:• A multi-domain social impact organization• A CSR implementation partner• A transparent and impact-driven NGO• A grassroots organization with scalable execution capability
Recommended Homepage Messaging
Heading:Creating Sustainable Social Impact Through Community-Led Development.Subheading:Aanav Seva Foundation is a Pune-based grassroots NGO working across health, nutrition, environment, education, and community empowerment through scalable and impact-driven initiatives.
Impact Snapshot Suggestions
• 4,700+ Girls Impacted• 2,500+ Women Reached• 80+ Children Supported Daily• 300+ Environmental Interventions• CSR Partnership with JSW Foundation
About Us Section
The About Us section should focus on:• Organizational philosophy• Grassroots approach• Transparency• Long-term impact• Community participation
Our Focus Areas
1. Women Health & Menstrual Hygiene2. Nutrition & Child Welfare3. Environment & Sustainability4. Art, Culture & Heritage5. Community Empowerment
Women Health & Menstrual Hygiene
Include:• Aditi initiative• Menstrual Hygiene Management Program• Rural awareness programs• Sanitary hygiene access initiatives
Nutrition & Child Welfare
Include Poshan Aahar initiative and nutrition support for needy and special children.
Environment & Sustainability
Include Kalpavruksha bamboo plantation initiative, carbon reduction activities, and environmental awareness programs.
Art, Culture & Heritage
Include Nrutyotsava initiative and activities related to preservation of Indian classical arts and culture.
CSR & Corporate Partnerships Page
Suggested Heading:Partnering for Scalable Social ImpactThis section should explain:• Why corporates should work with Aanav Seva Foundation• Execution capability• Monitoring and reporting systems• Grassroots outreach• Scalability and measurable impact
Projects Page
Projects should be presented as proof of execution capability and measurable impact. The JSW Foundation Menstrual Hygiene Management Program should be detailed here as a flagship case study.
Impact Page
Include:• Impact numbers• Case studies• Before/after metrics• Beneficiary stories• Community testimonials
Transparency & Compliance Page
Include:• CSR-1 Registration• 12A & 80G Certificates• PAN Details• NGO Registration• Annual Reports• Financial Transparency Information
Strategic Recommendation
The website should communicate that Aanav Seva Foundation is both compassionate and execution-oriented. The JSW Foundation partnership should be used as a credibility anchor and not as the entire identity of the organization.
