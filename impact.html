Beneficiary Case Studies

Women Health Success

Empowering Young Girls: Aditi's Story

"Before the Aanav Seva Foundation launched its Menstrual Hygiene Management camps in our school, missing 4-5 days of classes every month was normal for me. I felt embarrassed to ask questions. Today, with the Aditi program, we receive high-quality sanitary napkins and open scientific guidance. I haven't missed a single day of school this semester!"
— Aditi (Age 14, Rural Pune School Student)

Poshan Aahar Success

Fostering Healthy Growth: Meera's Journey

"As a single mother working as a domestic helper, ensuring proper daily nutrition for my special-needs child was extremely difficult. The Poshan Aahar initiative by the Aanav Seva Foundation has changed everything. The fresh, custom-cooked hygienic lunch box delivered to him every afternoon gives me deep peace of mind. The doctors verified that his weight and focus have improved dramatically."
— Smt. Meera (Mother of special-needs child)
