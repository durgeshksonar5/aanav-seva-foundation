Partnering for Scalable Social Impact

At the Aanav Seva Foundation, we believe corporate partnerships can multiply impact, scale interventions, and transform lives. Our foundation is a grounded, transparent, and proven implementation partner with the capability to execute meaningful social programs across women's hygiene, child nutrition, cultural preservation, and environmental conservation in Maharashtra.

Why Corporates Partner with Aanav Seva Foundation

We provide corporate entities with complete peace of mind through strict Section 135 compliance, transparent financial accounting, and verifiable field audits. With our active NGO registration status, CIN (U74999PN2018NPL178652), PAN (AARCA2837M), and Income Tax exemptions under Section 12AA and 80G, we ensure that every rupee of your CSR funds delivers tangible, documented social value that is easily traceable and ready for statutory reporting.

Grounded Grassroots Outreach

Operating directly at the grassroots in rural Pune and across Maharashtra since 2018, we bridge the gap between corporate resources and high-need communities. Our field officers reside within or near our operational zones, securing immediate access, local trust, and direct alignment with village panchayats and school administrators.

Execution Capability & Infrastructure

Our execution capability is backed by structural agreements, municipal permissions, and a trained operational team. A prime example of our capability is the **JSW Foundation Menstrual Hygiene Management Program**, which we launched and scaled to cover 20+ municipal schools, distributing over 50,000+ hygiene products with 98% verified adaptation rates.

Rigorous Monitoring & Reporting Systems

We eliminate ambiguity with digital monitoring. Every distribution drive is geo-tagged, and progress reports are updated on centralized databases. We provide our corporate sponsors with monthly progress audits, CA-certified fund utilization certificates, and detailed impact spreadsheets showing itemized expenditures.

Scalability & Measurable Impact

Our programs are designed as structured modules that can be replicated and scaled across multiple regions. From local child nutrition centers supporting special-needs children to geo-tagged bamboo plantations, we map baseline parameters against verified post-campaign metrics to demonstrate clean, measurable social return on investment.
